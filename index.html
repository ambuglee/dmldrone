About

With over 25 years of experience as a licensed and insured commercial drone pilot, I possess extensive expertise in flying both fixed wing and remote aircraft. Additionally, my background as a retired Peace Officer adds an extra layer of professionalism and attention to detail to my services.

At my Drone Photography service, I specialize in capturing stunning aerial images and videos for commercial and real estate purposes, as well as conducting thorough roof inspections. My focus is on delivering exceptional quality while keeping my services highly affordable. By choosing my service, you can expect top-notch visuals that effectively showcase the properties you are marketing. Moreover, for roof and solar panel inspections, I provide valuable insights into potential issues, empowering you to make informed decisions regarding necessary repairs or maintenance

If you would like to discover how my service can benefit you or schedule an inspection, please don't hesitate to reach out to me through my inquiry page. I am Dale M. Lee, the proud owner of this enterprise, and I look forward to serving you.
